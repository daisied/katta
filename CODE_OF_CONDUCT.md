# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; critique code, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on technical outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of community discussion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report incidents through GitHub by contacting `@daisied` directly.
